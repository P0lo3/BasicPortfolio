About Me

I kicked off my career in Managed Services at a large ISP, where I saw how the right systems could revolutionize efficiency and data management. In that role, I focused on meeting Service Level Agreements (SLAs) and ensuring seamless operations for our customers. Over time, my curiosity pulled me toward programming—I started tinkering with various languages to weave them into our systems, automating data collection and patching up gaps in our Configuration Management Database (CMDB). That experience ignited my passion for web development, leading me to dive into full-stack development, where I can shape and optimize applications from the ground up. Now, I’m eager to bring my skills and problem-solving mindset to a full-stack developer role.
