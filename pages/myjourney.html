My Profesional Journey

My journey started in Managed Services at a large ISP, where I realized that the right systems could make a huge difference in efficiency and data management.

My role was all about making sure Service Level Agreements (SLAs) were met and that everything ran smoothly for our customers.

Over time, I became more interested in programming. I began experimenting with different languages to integrate them into our systems, helping automate data collection and fix gaps in how we were capturing and storing data, especially in our Configuration Management Database (CMDB).

This led me to pursue web development, where I could directly impact how applications are built and optimized.
