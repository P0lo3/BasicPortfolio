Let me ask the following question...

"Ever searched for a product or service online and get a list of options? You're glad to find some choices, but when you click to check out their website—whether for pricing, details, or just to see what they're about—you realize they don’t even have one.

People want to feel confident about the business or person that they engage with. By having a website this helps people make an informed decision and gives them a sense of trustworthiness.
